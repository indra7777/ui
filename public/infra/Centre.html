Description

A centre lathe is a versatile machine tool used primarily for shaping metal or wood. It works by rotating the workpiece on its axis while a cutting tool moves parallel to the axis of rotation to produce precise cylindrical or conical shapes. The machine is essential for operations like turning, facing, threading, and drilling.
