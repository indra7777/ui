Description

A tubular furnace is a high-temperature heating device with a cylindrical cavity, ideal for materials processing and heat treatment. It features precise temperature control and uniform heating along its length. The furnace is commonly used for sintering, annealing, and thermal analysis of materials in controlled environments.
