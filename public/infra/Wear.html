Description

A wear testing machine is used to evaluate the wear behavior and friction characteristics of materials. It typically operates on the pin-on-disc principle where a pin (or ball) is pressed against a rotating disc under controlled conditions. This allows researchers to study material wear rates, friction coefficients, and surface degradation under various loads and speeds.
